Digital Service Team Manifesto

We are uncovering better ways of developing software by following design principles and the service manual in order to meet the Government Digital By Default Service Standard.

Through this work we have come to value:

Agile development culture

over prescriptive processes.

Open source, standards and data

over closed proprietary systems and formats.

User needs and research

over business requirements and models.

Empowered multidisciplinary teams

over segregated silos of knowledge.


That is, while we used to value the items on the right, we now value the items on the left more.
