Digital Service Team Manifesto

We are practicing better ways of developing software by following the design principles and service manual in order to pass the digital by default service standard. Through this work we have come to value:

Agile development culture over prescriptive processes.

Open source, standards and data over closed proprietary systems and formats.

User needs and research over business requirements and models.

Empowered multidisciplinary teams over segregated silos of knowledge.


That is, while we used to value the items on the right, we now value the items on the left more.
